Leonardo da Vinci

The most intelligent human in history

Timeline

April 15, 1452: Leonardo da Vinci is born in Vinci, Italy

1452

1467: Leonardo moves to Florence and begins his apprenticeship with Andrea del Verrocchio, a leading artist and sculptor of the time.

1467

1472: Leonardo becomes a master in the Guild of Saint Luke, the guild of artists and doctors of medicine in Florence.

1472

1482-1499: While in Milan, Leonardo works on various projects, including The Last Supper and multiple scientific sketches and inventions.

1482

1495-1498: Leonardo works on The Last Supper, one of his most famous paintings, located in the convent of Santa Maria delle Grazie in Milan.

1495

1503-1506: Leonardo begins working on the Mona Lisa, which would become one of the most iconic portraits in history.

1503

1513: Leonardo moves to Rome, where he works under the patronage of Pope Leo X. He focuses on scientific and anatomical studies during this time.

1513

May 2, 1519: Leonardo da Vinci dies in Amboise, France, at the age of 67.

1519
